We have a Winner!

The Gosport Community Lottery is up and away. The maiden draw was made on Saturday evening. The winning numbers were 259133. (You have to match the first or last 2-6 numbers in a row to win.) We are delighted to announce that one of our supporters was a winner!

If you haven’t bought a ticket yet you can always sign up here: https://www.gosportcommunitylottery.co.uk/support/the-diving-museum

The next draw will be on Saturday 15th September. We wish you all the best of luck – and thank you so much for your support.
